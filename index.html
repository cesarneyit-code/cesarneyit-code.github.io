I am a professor in the Department of Mathematics at Universidad de los Andes (Bogotá, Colombia).

I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics from Universidad Nacional de Córdoba (Argentina, 2009) and my B.Sc. in Mathematics from Universidad Nacional de Colombia (2005).

Research

My work is centered on the structure and classification of fusion and braided tensor categories, their connections to topological quantum field theories, and applications to topological quantum computation.

  • Fusion and braided fusion categories: Structure, classification, and modular data of fusion categories, including weakly integral, super-modular, and \(G\)-crossed settings.
  • Topological quantum field theory: Categorical symmetries, Turaev–Viro and Reshetikhin–Turaev invariants, modular extensions, and topological phases.
  • Quantum computation: Braid group representations from fusion categories, anyonic models, and quantum error correction in Kitaev's model.
